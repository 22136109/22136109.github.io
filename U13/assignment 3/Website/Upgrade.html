Upgrading your phone allows you to move to a newer device before or at the end of your contract. Most providers allow upgrades once you have completed a minimum contract period, usually 12 or 24 months. You can either pay off the remaining balance on your current device or trade it in to reduce the cost of your new phone. Upgrading ensures you stay up to date with the latest technology, improved cameras, better battery life and faster performance.

Upgrade with Apple

Apple offers upgrade options through their iPhone Upgrade Programme, allowing customers to switch to the newest iPhone each year.
